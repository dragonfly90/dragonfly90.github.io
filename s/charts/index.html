Introduction to Graph Reading Experiment

In this experiment, you will be asked questions about information presented in graphical form. For each trial in the experiment, you will first see a question. When you have understood the question, you will press the Enter key on the keyboard. You will then see a chart or line graph. When you have determined the answer to the question, you will press the Enter key, and then you will choose your answer. You will then see a chart or line graph.
